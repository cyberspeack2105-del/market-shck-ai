How our AI Engine Works

The core of MarketShock AI is a proprietary ensemble model that fuses historical job market data with forward-looking technological projections. Transparency is our priority.

Prediction Architecture

graph TD A[Job Listing Data] --> D[Forecaster Engine] B[Layoff Trends] --> D C[AI 2030 Projections] --> D D --> E{Analysis Layer} E --> F[Shock Risk Score] E --> G[Growth Velocity] E --> H[Market Absorption] F & G & H --> I[Consolidated Insight]

Shock Risk Score

0-100

A composite metric measuring the likelihood of structural disruption within an industry. It takes into account:

  • Volume of historical layoffs vs industry size
  • Current hiring momentum (ARIMA trends)
  • AI automation probability by 2030

Market Absorption

Percentage

Measures how "integrated" an industry is with technological shifts. High absorption suggests the industry is effectively adopting AI to improve productivity.

  • Skill density in job listings
  • Historical resilience during data shocks

Ethical Artificial Intelligence

We believe in "Augmented Intelligence" rather than pure replacement. Our models are trained to identify where human skills can be complimented by technology, providing a roadmap for career transition and industry evolution rather than just forecasting negative outcomes.
